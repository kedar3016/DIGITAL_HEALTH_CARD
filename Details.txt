Problem Statment : Managing patient health records manually is time-consuming and inefficient.
A digital health card system is required to store and access patient, doctor, and lab information securely and easily.  



Aim : The aim of this project is to design and develop a Digital Health Card System that securely stores and manages
patient health information, doctor details, and laboratory records in a centralized digital platform. 
The system aims to provide easy access to medical data, improve communication between patients, doctors, and laboratories, 
reduce paperwork, and enhance the efficiency and quality of health.



Objective : To design and develop a centralized digital system for storing patient health records securely.
            To maintain doctor information including specialization, qualification, and experience.
	    To manage laboratory details and laboratory test records efficiently.
            To provide easy and quick access to patient medical information for authorized users.
            To reduce manual paperwork and eliminate data redundancy in healthcare records.
            To improve coordination and communication between patients, doctors, and laboratories.
            To ensure data accuracy, security, and privacy of healthcare information.
            To make the healthcare system more efficient, reliable, and user-friendly.    



Module : Patient Module
         Doctor Module
         Lab Module
         Admin Module

                 View doctor and lab details
                 Book appointments
                 View lab reports

                View patient details
